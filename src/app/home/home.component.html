Our Services

Maternity

Comprehensive care for expectant mothers, including prenatal care, childbirth, and postnatal support.

Gynaecology

Specialized care for women's reproductive health issues, including menstrual disorders, fertility concerns, and gynecological surgeries.

Pediatric Care

Medical services tailored to meet the unique healthcare needs of infants, children, and adolescents.

Radiology

Diagnostic imaging services, such as X-rays, MRI, and ultrasound, to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions.

Nutrition and Dietetics

Personalized dietary guidance and nutrition counseling to promote overall health and well-being.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation and physical therapy services to improve mobility, reduce pain, and enhance quality of life.

Breastfeeding Support

Education, guidance, and lactation consultation to assist new mothers in breastfeeding their infants successfully.

NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit)

Specialized medical care for premature or critically ill newborns, providing advanced monitoring and treatment.

Stem Cell Banking

Preservation and storage of umbilical cord blood and tissue for potential future medical use.
